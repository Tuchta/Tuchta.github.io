Welcome to my portfolio, where my unique journey and diverse experiences have culminated in a passion for data analytics and computer science.

Shortly after finishing my Master's degree in Aeronautical Engineering and working in the Specialty Coffee industry, I realized that I needed to pivot in a new direction. It wasn't easy to acknowledge this realization, but I knew I needed to take a leap of faith to find my true calling. That's when I discovered about Harvard published CS50's Introduction to Computer Science course, which opened my eyes to the world of programming languages and their immense importance.

After exploring the job market, I became fascinated by the potential of data analytics and its importance to businesses, so I decided to apply for the Data Analytics course at Turing College. At Turing College, I gained a comprehensive understanding of data and the working principles. After a bit, I became a Junior Team Lead, where I gained invaluable knowledge by helping my peers.

I am proud of what I have accomplished so far and am excited to see where my passion for data will take me next. Please feel free to check out my LinkedIn account for more information on my background, skills, and achievements.
